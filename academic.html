Spring 2025

Class Field Theory

  • Week 1. G-modules, functor of G-invariants and its cohomological extension, coinduced modules, standard complex. Isaac
  • Week 2. Standard complex, group homology, connecting maps for first cohomology and homology, change of groups, dimension shifting. Sudharshan
  • Week 3. Inflation-Restriction exact sequence, Tate cohomology groups. Isaac
  • Week 4. Tate groups, cup products. Sudharshan
  • Week 5. Cohomological triviality. Sudharshan
  • Week 6. Herbrand quotient, Tate's Theorem. Isaac

Automorphic Forms

  • Week 1. Overview of Tate's Thesis. Leo
  • Week 2-3. Local Theory: Zeta functions, functional equation, multiplicity one.
  • Week 4. Local Theory: Computing Zeta values for specific functions, Riemann-Roch from Poisson summation. Trailer for Global Theory.
  • Week 5. Global theory. Additive and multiplicative fundamental domains. Compactness of adelic quotient is Dirichlet's unit theorem + finiteness of class number. Global functional equations, L-factors.
  • Week 6. Global functional equation. Determining the sign of the Gauss sum using quadratic reciprocity and Tate's thesis. Gamma factors, significance of root numbers. Functional equation for L-series.
  • Week 7. Eisenstein series. Intertwining operator on L2 Automorphic-forms. Discrete and continuous spectra. Leo
  • Week 8. Real analytic Eisenstein series. Computing the Fourier coefficients and constant terms. Functional equation. Yash
  • Week 9. Modular forms as Automorphic Forms. Definition of Automorphic forms on adelic points of an algebraic group. Finiteness of the dimension of space of automorphic forms.
